Working with colors

Color contrast

Color contrast is the difference in light between font and background colors. It is important for readability and accessibility.

WCAG 2.0 requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text.

Use the WebAIM contrast checker to check the contrast ratio of your colors.

Color blindness

Color blindness is a vision deficiency that affects the perception of color. It is important to consider color blindness when designing websites.

Use the Toptal color filter tool to simulate color blindness.
